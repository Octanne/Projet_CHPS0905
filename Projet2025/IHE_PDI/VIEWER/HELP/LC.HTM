WINDOWING


• To adjust luminosity and/or contrast of all images, right click in image zone and keep the left mouse button pressed while moving the mouse :

- Vertically to adjust luminosity
- Horizontally to adjust contrast

 • You can also adjust those values only on selected images by keeping the Shift button pressed while windowing.

• To reset luminosity and contrast to default values, left click on this button

• To invert grey levels (negative/positive), left click on the following button
• STANDARD WINDOWINGS

In the list of TOOLS and FUNCTIONS, you can select preset windowings specific to anatomic zones.
